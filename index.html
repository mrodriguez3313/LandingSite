How is mutable data on IPFS possible?

IPNSGoServer uses the Interplanetary Naming Service (IPNS) under the hood. IPNS is like these storage units. One is appointed to you and you can change what is inside at anytime. Now imagine that the other side has an unbreakable glass window; others can see your content, but only you have the key to change it.
